Questions we get asked.

What's the difference between the two programs?

Founder AI Program is scoped around you and your leadership team — your own AI fluency and strategy. Team AI Program is org-wide: the adoption, training, and automation that gets the whole company using it.

Can we start with just one capability?

Yes — each capability in the grid above is also a standalone entry point. Most clients start narrow and expand into a full program once the first piece proves out.

How long does an engagement run?

We haven't standardized a fixed term yet — it's scoped to what you're solving for. Ask on a call rather than assuming a template timeline.

Do you build custom software, or just advise?

Both, depending on the capability. AI Agents & Workflow Automation and Personal AI Assistants involve real build work, not just recommendations.
