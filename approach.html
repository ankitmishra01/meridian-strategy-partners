Questions we get asked.

Why not hire a big-name consultancy instead?

Scale, mostly — a big firm gives you a partner for the sales call and analysts for the work. Here, the founders do the work themselves. That's a trade-off: less bench depth, more direct accountability and speed.

What does an engagement actually cost?

We haven't published fixed pricing — Parallel AI is new enough that we'd rather scope it to your situation on a call than quote a number that doesn't fit. Ask the concierge or book a call for a real conversation.

Do you work with any industry, or a specific niche?

Ankit's direct background is fintech and cleantech, but the method — diagnose, design, deploy, sustain — isn't industry-specific. If your team has AI tools and no adoption, or no strategy at all, that's the actual qualifying signal.

What if we're not ready for a full engagement?

Start with the free Readiness Check — four questions, no email, a live AI-generated read on where you stand. It's designed to tell you honestly if the timing is wrong, not just sell you in.
